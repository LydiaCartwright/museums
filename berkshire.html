About the Berkshire

The Berkshire Museum combines art, science, and history to create thought-provoking experiences for visitors of all ages.

The museum’s diverse collection of more than 40,000 objects is brought to life year round through exhibitions, educational programming,
digital content, and community events. We truly are your community museum, offering access to education and culture to the residents of
Pittsfield, the Berkshires, and beyond.
